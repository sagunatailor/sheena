Terms and Conditions for All-You-Can-Eat Buffet

  1. **Dining Time Limit**: Each guest is allocated a dining time of 90 minutes.
  2. **No Sharing**: Food from the buffet is for dine-in consumption only. Sharing with non-paying guests or taking food outside the premises is strictly prohibited.
  3. **No Leftovers**: Please take only what you can eat to minimize waste. Excessive leftovers may result in an additional charge.
  4. **Children's Policy**: Children must be supervised at all times. Buffet pricing for children is based on age brackets.
  5. **Health and Safety**: For the health and safety of all guests, please use the provided utensils .
  6. **Behaviour**: Guests are expected to behave in a respectful and orderly manner. Management reserves the right to refuse service to anyone behaving inappropriately.
  7. **Food Availability**: While we strive to keep all dishes available, some items may run out or be replaced without prior notice.
  8. **Allergies and Dietary Restrictions**: Please inform our staff of any allergies or dietary restrictions before dining. We do not guarantee the absence of allergens.
  9. **Management Rights**: Management reserves the right to modify these terms and conditions at any time without prior notice.
  10. By entering the buffet, guests agree to abide by these terms and conditions. Failure to comply may result in removal from the premises and potential legal action.
